# ADR-0008 — Immutable Decisions and Acceptance

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-13

## Decision

Decisions, approvals, evidence, checks, risk acceptances, overrides, and acceptance are append-only
facts bound to explicit scope and revisions. Changes use supersession, revocation, amendment, or
a new revision.

## Consequences

Claims and successful process exits cannot become completion without current checks, evidence,
and owner acceptance.
